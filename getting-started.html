Chapter 07 — The protocol

Getting Started

An 8-step protocol to enter ketosis cleanly and efficiently. Follow this exactly — the rewards show up by week 2.

1

Calculate your macros

Use the calculator on the macros page to find your daily targets for fat, protein, and carbs. Write them down. These are your north star.

2

Clean out your kitchen

Remove or donate all bread, pasta, rice, sugar, juice, cereal, and packaged snacks. You cannot out-discipline an environment working against you.

3

Stock up on essentials

Eggs, butter, olive oil, fatty meats, salmon, avocados, leafy greens, cheese, almonds, coffee, heavy cream. See the foods list.

4

Buy electrolytes before you start

Real salt (Redmond's, Celtic, pink), magnesium glycinate (300mg capsules), potassium chloride (Nu-Salt or LoSalt). Day 1 will be easier.

5

Track for the first 2 weeks

Use an app like Cronometer or Carb Manager. Weigh your food. It's tedious — and essential for learning your new baseline. You'll stop tracking once your intuition catches up.

6

Consider a 24-hour fast to kickstart

Optional but effective. A single-day fast before starting keto depletes glycogen fast and gets you into ketosis by day 2 instead of day 4.

7

Measure progress — beyond the scale

Waist circumference, energy levels, sleep quality, mental clarity, mood. The scale is a lagging indicator. Body composition shifts faster than body weight on keto.

8

Give it 6 weeks before judging

Full fat-adaptation takes 4–6 weeks. Many people quit in week 2 — right before it gets magical. Stick it out. Energy, appetite control, and fat loss all compound after week 3.

You have everything you need.

Macros. Food lists. Electrolyte protocol. Research to back it up. The rest is up to you.
