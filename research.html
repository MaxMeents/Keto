Chapter 05 — Latest findings

What's new in 2025–2026

The frontier of ketogenic research — from neurology to endocrinology. A curated snapshot of the most significant recent publications.

APR 2025 Stanford Medicine

Keto Therapy & Mental Illness

Stanford researchers detail how ketogenic therapy regulates immune signaling and reduces oxidative stress — offering a metabolic pathway to treat depression, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. BHB appears to act directly on the brain's inflammatory machinery.

neurosciencepsychiatryinflammation
2025 Science Advances

Long-term Keto in Mice: A Caution

A long-term ad libitum ketogenic diet in mice induced hyperlipidemia, hepatic dysfunction, and impaired insulin secretion. A reminder that duration, context, and food quality matter — and that mouse models don't always translate to humans.

metabolismriskspreclinical
2025 Nutrients

Keto vs Other Weight-Loss Diets

A comparative review finds ketogenic diets outperform low-fat diets for short-term fat loss and glycemic control, with comparable long-term outcomes when adherence is matched between groups.

weight lossmeta-analysis
2025 PMC Scoping Review

Symptoms at Keto Initiation

A systematic review of "keto flu" — occurrence rates, underlying mechanisms, and evidence-based relief. Electrolyte repletion (sodium, potassium, magnesium) is confirmed as the primary intervention.

side effectsclinical
2024–25 Nature STTT

Mechanisms in Human Disease

A comprehensive review of how ketogenic metabolism influences cancer, cardiovascular disease, autoimmune conditions, and neurodegeneration at the molecular level. Published in Signal Transduction and Targeted Therapy.

mechanismdiseasereview
2025 RCT Crossover

Alzheimer's & Modified Keto

Patients on a modified ketogenic diet achieved sustained physiological ketosis with improved daily living scores (ADCS-ADL) and quality-of-life metrics (QOL-AD) versus their usual diet.

alzheimer'sRCT
2023 Nutrients Umbrella Review

Keto & Multiple Health Outcomes

An umbrella review of meta-analyses covering dozens of ketogenic trials. Consistent signal for weight loss, glycemic benefit, seizure reduction, and emerging mental-health applications.

umbrella reviewoverview
2021 Nature STTT

Underlying Mechanisms

A foundational review cataloging how ketogenic diets modulate human disease at the molecular level — mitochondrial biogenesis, epigenetic regulation via HDAC inhibition, and gut microbiome shifts.

mechanismfoundation
