Chapter 02 — Precision nutrition

The 75 · 20 · 5 Rule

The standard ketogenic diet is defined by strict macronutrient ratios. Too little fat, and you're hungry. Too much protein, and you'll be knocked out of ketosis. Get these right — and ketosis becomes inevitable.

75% fat

Fat

The fuel source. Avocado, olive oil, fatty fish, butter, eggs, nuts, and fatty cuts of meat.

~165g daily • based on 2000 kcal
20% protein

Protein

Moderate. Excess protein converts to glucose via gluconeogenesis — enough for muscle, not more.

~75g daily • based on 2000 kcal
5% carbs

Carbs

Strict. Under 20–50g net carbs daily, from leafy greens and low-sugar vegetables only.

~20–40g daily • net carbs
